This Cookie Policy explains how Tydli.io ("we", "us", "the service")

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a
website. They help websites function and provide analytics or
preferences.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

### 2.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the service to function: - Authentication
cookies (to keep you logged in) - Security and rate-limit protection -
Session management

You cannot opt out of essential cookies.

### 2.2. Analytics Cookies (PostHog)

We use PostHog to understand how users interact with the service.
Analytics cookies may record: - Pages visited - Buttons clicked -
Feature usage - Device/browser data

Analytics are only enabled: - With your consent (required for EU/EEA/UK
users) - Or when strictly necessary and configured in a
privacy-preserving manner

PostHog is not used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

The following third parties may place cookies or process cookie-like
data: - **Google** (authentication) - **PostHog** (analytics) -
**Supabase/Lovable Cloud** (session/access tokens)

## 4. Managing Cookies

You may control or delete cookies using your browser settings. You may
also withdraw your consent for analytics at any time, where applicable.

Disabling essential cookies may prevent the service from functioning
correctly.

## 5. Updates

posted with a new "Last updated" date.
