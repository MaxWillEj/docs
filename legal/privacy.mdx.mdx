Last updated: \\[Insert Date\\]

applicable laws.

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate [Tydli.io](http://Tydli.io).

### 1.1. Information you provide

- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- Any content you upload or enter into your deployments
- Optional profile information (e.g., display name)

### 1.2. Information collected automatically

- Basic device and browser information
- Usage data through PostHog analytics (e.g., pages visited, clicks,

  feature usage)
- IP address for security, logging, and rate limiting

We do not store API request/response payloads or any third-party API

data unless you explicitly provide it through the dashboard.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

- Provide, maintain, and secure the service
- Authenticate your account
- Operate MCP deployments
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Analyze product usage using privacy-respecting analytics
- Communicate about account-related matters (verification, security

  alerts)

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

## 3. Legal Bases (GDPR)

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

- Performance of a contract: creating your account, providing access

  to [Tydli.io](http://Tydli.io), securely storing credentials.
- Legitimate interests: improving the service, detecting abuse,

  ensuring security, operating analytics with minimal personal data.
- Consent: analytics cookies or tracking technologies where legally

  required (e.g., EU/EEA users).

You may withdraw consent at any time.

## 4. Third-Party Service Providers

### 4.1. Google (Authentication / SSO)

If you sign in with Google, Google shares your email address, name,

Google account ID, and (optionally) your profile picture. You may revoke

access at any time through your Google Account settings.

### 4.2. PostHog (Product Analytics)

We use PostHog to understand how [Tydli.io](http://Tydli.io) is used and to improve

functionality. PostHog may collect: - Page views - Button clicks -

Feature usage - Basic device information - IP address (short-term)

We do not use PostHog for advertising or cross-site tracking.

### 4.3. Lovable Cloud (Backend Hosting)

[Tydli.io](http://Tydli.io) runs on Lovable Cloud, which processes database operations,

user accounts, deployments, and backend logic.

### 4.4. Supabase (Database, Auth, Storage, Edge Functions)

Lovable Cloud uses Supabase infrastructure for encrypted credential

storage, authentication, RLS enforcement, database hosting, object

storage, and audit logging.

## 5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Analytics cookies (PostHog)

Analytics cookies are only set with consent where legally required.

- Row-Level Security ensures isolation

Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
